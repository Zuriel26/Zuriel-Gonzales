Healthy Body & Environment


In the modern way of living, it is difficult to prevent the risks of diseases most particularly Covid-19. The lifestyle
of many young individuals may negatively affect their health. As I observed many have impaired immune system
compared with those with healthy body and lifestyle. These were the people with life balance and well aware about
the advantages of having a healthy body. They knew that to prevent from other diseases or Covid-19 they must
boost their immune system through eating healthy foods, doing exercises, adequate water intake, good sleeping habits,
sanitation, good hygiene and healthy and safe workplace. It is important too that we stay connected to someone we love.
Receiving positive attitude and sharing happy memories can help our body and mind at ease and become healthy.The
learnings that I got from Covid-19 pandemic are having a healthy body and environment are the most important aspect
that we should consider to prevent from the virus or other diseases.
